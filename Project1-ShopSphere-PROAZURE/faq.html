Frequently Asked Questions

1. How long does delivery take?

Delivery usually takes 3-7 business days.

2. Do you offer Cash on Delivery?

Yes, Cash on Delivery is available for selected locations.

3. Are products genuine?

Yes, all products sold on ShopSphere are 100% genuine.

4. How can I track my order?

Once your order is shipped, a tracking link will be sent to your registered email address and mobile number.

5. Can I cancel my order?

Yes, orders can be cancelled before they are shipped. After shipping, cancellation requests may not be accepted.\

6. What payment methods are accepted?

We accept Credit Cards, Debit Cards, UPI, Net Banking, Wallets, and Cash on Delivery.

7. Do products come with a warranty?

Most products include manufacturer warranties. Warranty details are available on individual product pages.
