Return Policy

ShopSphere Return Policy

Customer satisfaction is our top priority. If you are not completely satisfied with your purchase, you may return eligible products under the following conditions.



Eligibility for Returns

  • Products must be returned within 7 days of delivery.
  • Items should be unused and in original condition.
  • Original packaging, tags, and accessories must be included.
  • Products damaged due to misuse are not eligible for returns.


Non-Returnable Products

  • Gift cards and downloadable software.
  • Personal care products after opening.
  • Customized or personalized products.


Refund Process

After receiving and inspecting the returned product, refunds will be initiated within 5 to 7 business days. Refunds will be credited through the original payment method.



Exchange Policy

Customers may request product replacement if they receive damaged, defective, or incorrect items.
